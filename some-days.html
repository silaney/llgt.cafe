Some Days

Some days
Just weary
Some days
Just dreary

Not on fire
Every day
Not excited
In every way

It may be sunny out
But not inside
I may be frowning
Or smiling inside

I’m not sure why
I feel as I do
I’m not quire sure
Why I do
The things I do

But I know this
That God is good
And he lives me me
And this is why
My life is good
Because
God lives in me
